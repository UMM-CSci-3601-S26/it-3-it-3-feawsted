Purchase List

Welcome to the purchase list! Here, you can view the type of items specified in student checklists, as well as the corresponding quantities for each item as far as what is needed, what is in inventory, and what will need to be purchased. A priority tab will also be provided for each item to organize item importance. Please note that this list is an estimate and doesn't account for specified item attributes when generating the purchase list, so some items may be over or under estimated. For example, if 10 students need a "red shirt" and 5 students need a "blue shirt", the purchase list will show that 15 shirts are needed, without accounting for the different colors. Please use the purchase list as a general guide, but be sure to check the student checklists for more specific details on the items needed until future updates are added.
