Purchase List

Welcome to the purchase list page! Here, you can view all items specified in student checklists, the quantity of those needed items, the quantity of those items in inventory, and the quantity that is needed to be purchased. Additionally, filtering by item and priority is provided, as well as a sort by feature for item quantities (needs, inventory, and purchase amounts).
